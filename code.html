MLDegreeLSSM

Related article: Carlos Améndola, Lukas Gustafsson, Kathlén Kohn, Orlando Marigliano, and Anna Seigal. On the maximum likelihood degree of linear spaces of symmetric matrices. In preparation.

This Macaulay2 code computes ML degrees of various linear spaces of symmetric matrices and demonstrates the computation of the ML degree via Segre classes. [link]
